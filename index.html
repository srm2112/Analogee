Analogee


Providing Solutions for Your Semiconductor Equipment Needs.

From the beginning, Analogee’s fundamental business objective has been to support its customers in achieving the best possible performance from their Matrix System One™ equipment. This objective continues to be achieved through the Analogee Operating System upgrade for the Matrix System One ™.

In an effort to broaden the impact we can provide for our customers, we have expanded our product offerings to include the AutoEtch 490 and 590 parts and repairs, along with repairs for various sub-assemblies for other systems. Please review our "Repairs" section for more details.

Solutions


When the cost of purchasing new and/or additional equipment is too expensive, the continued use of existing systems typically prevails. However, Analogee routinely provides a budgetary solution by upgrading your legacy equipment with our field proven operating system upgrade and subassembly solutions.


Upgrades

We upgrade original Matrix Systems with our operating system, which allows the user to have better control over their process, improved reliability, and reduced maintenance time. In addition, we offer re-designed sub-assemblies for the Matrix System One Lines..

Parts

We offer standard and improved replacement parts along with consumable parts for the Matrix System One and the AutoEtch 490 and 590.

Repairs

We provide repair and calibration services for various semiconductor sub-assemblies such as: manometers, isolation valves, throttle valves, RF generators, matching networks, and various system circuit boards. Please visit our “Repairs” section to see the various manufacturers we support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide service for matching networks?
Yes, we offer repair services and upgrades for various matching network manufacturers and models.

Do you have field service support?
Yes, our field support technician has over 30 years of experience with Matrix Systems and has been supporting the Analogee tool since its inception.

What type of warranty do you offer on your products?
We offer warranties on both our new and refurbished parts. The warranty varies depending on the product.

Do you have parts available for both Matrix Strippers and Etchers?
Yes, we support and have parts available for both Matrix Strippers and Etchers.

Are you associated with other tools besides the Matrix Systems?
Yes, we also supply parts and offer repairs for the AutoEtch 490 and 590 systems. In addition, we provide repair services for various manufacturers of matching networks, RF generators, manometers, throttle valves, and isolation valves. Please see our list under the “Repairs” section of our website.

Do I need an RMA number to have a product repaired?
Yes, please contact us to request an RMA number for your item to be repaired. The RMA number allows us to track the item during the repair process and can provide a history about the item if it has been repaired by us previously.

About Us

Analogee was established as a California based business entity in 1999. Our founders have more than 50 years of combined experience in the semiconductor process equipment industry; including engineering, manufacturing, applications, service, sales, marketing, and corporate management.

Analogee relocated its headquarters to Boise, Idaho, in 2012. This relocation has provided Analogee the opportunity to better serve our customers and expand our business. The facility is staffed with experienced customer oriented engineering, technical, sales, and service team members.

We continue to use our expertise to offer you the best products for your semi-conductor solutions.
